# Training Records

This directory contains **templates for training records**.

Training records provide:
- Objective evidence of completed education
- Traceability between training plans and verification
- Responsibility acknowledgment by trainees

Records in this directory support:
- Internal audits
- Management review
- Continuous improvement activities

Attendance alone is not sufficient; records must show **verified competence**.
